Data Analysis with R

Private companies and research institutions capture terabytes of data about their users’ interactions, business, social media, and also sensors from devices such as mobile phones and automobiles. The challenge of this era is to make sense of this sea of data. This is where data analytics comes into the picture. R is an integrated suite of software facilities for data manipulation, calculation and graphical display. This program will guide the learners to use the R language for analysing a dataset and solving machine learning algorithms.

Robotic Process Automation

Robotic Process Automation is one of the booming industries in today’s digitally driven economy. It mimics the way humans interact with software to perform repeatable tasks of high volume. With RPA, a business can automate tiresome rule-based tasks thereby, enabling the business users to devote more time to other higher-value work. This program helps the learners to gain hands-on experience in creating software bots and automating repeated processes.
